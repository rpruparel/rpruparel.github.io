   UNCC Eats

Developing a proximity based Internet of Things (IoT) Android app for Chartwells by Compass Group USA at University of North Carolina at Charlotte that uses Bluetooth Beacons to tracks users at the food outlets across University and send coupons in push notification to improve businesses and enhance user's experience. This app can track user's food purchases at the cash counter and can also track user's passing by the different food outlets.
